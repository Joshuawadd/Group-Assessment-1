High Performance Computing


High Performace computing is the use of parrellelo computing resources to solve difficult problems in a reasonable amount of time High preformace computing can come in many diffrent forms, from Super Computers to GPGPUs these all fall under the heading of "High preformance computing devices". All though the distribution of these systems and devices is very intresting the problems they can help us solve are countless and can help shape our every day lives.

Numerical Weather Prediction
Using mathmatical models we can simulate the behaviour of our atmosphere and oceans to predict the Weather. The chaotic nature of the atmosphere is modeled by partial differential equations. These equations have no exact solutions hence need to be solved numerically. Solving these equations numberical is not an easy thing to do and is a task under taken by some of the most powerful super computers in the world.
Computational Chemistry
Predicting and synthesising new chemicals is a time consuming and costly process often involving a lot of trial and error. However chemical interations can be predicted from large infomation sets of known interations and detailed simulations. These simulations can be very detailed and very complex and benifit from GPGPU. After using using computation to find theoretically viable options, chemists can test those options saving time and money and allowing chemical to be discovered that couldn't be discovered before
