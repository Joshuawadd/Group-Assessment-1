Computing and Communication

Computing has changed the way people in society communicate with each other. There are various improvements that the development of computers has brought to communication that benefit society as a whole. Some of the main improvements are:

  • Speed - The introduction of instant messaging has meant that people can send messages and talk with each immediately. This allows for people to increase efficiency when working together, as information and ideas can be communicated faster and easier.
  • Audience - The development of social media has allowed mass communication to develop. People can reach and speak to more people than ever, and entertain them, educate them or share their ideas.
  • Interaction - Online communication has created more ways for people to communicate. Chat rooms, forums and video calling mean that people have more ways than ever to communicate with other people, especially over long distances.
